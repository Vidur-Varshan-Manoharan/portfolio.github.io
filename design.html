    My Designs

    • Utility Knife

      A durable, ergonomic utility knife designed and 3D printed for everyday use. Created to explore functional product design and the durability of PLA, this project helped me refine my understanding of mechanical joints, blade safety integration, and real-world prototyping using Fusion 360.


    • Vikings Horn Holder

      A customized display stand engineered to showcase a Viking-themed horn. This design combined aesthetics with structural integrity and was created to experiment with artistic styling, symmetry, and part stability in 3D prints.


    • Insulin Pen Portable Case

      A protective, travel-friendly case designed specifically for the Allstar insulin pen. This project focused on user-centric design, optimizing internal fit, snap-fit features, and print orientation to ensure strength, accessibility, and compactness.


    • Flower Pot

      A decorative yet functional flower pot designed with smooth organic curves and optimized water drainage. The pot was designed for printability without supports and to experiment with form aesthetics in interior objects.
