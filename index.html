Camellia Sinensis / Ananas Comosus Fiber Based Uni-Directional Corrugated Panel for Sound Damping Application. (Academic)


Executed a groundbreaking project where innovation meets sustainability in the realm of sound damping technology. Our mission was to engineer an environmentally friendly sound damping panel tailored for higher frequency applications, revolutionizing the industry with a unique approach. By harnessing the natural fibers, we crafted a uni-directional corrugated panel structure embedded within epoxy resin. The plates developed in corrugated shape and molded through mold pressing method. In collaboration with esteemed mentor Gokul Kumar. S and dedicated peers Reavanth. S and Suryaprakash. A, we successfully patented this eco-friendly panel, marking a significant milestone in sustainable sound damping solutions.
Highlights:

  • The panel is made with Camellia sinensis (tea plant) and Ananas comosus (pineapple), added along with E-Glass Fibres to increase strength.
  • The panel was ingeniously designed featuring a sinusoidal corrugatedpattern sandwiched betweenflat fiber plates.
  • The NFRHCs (Natural Fibre Reinforced Hybrid Composites) are planned with a 5 mm thickness and 7 plies, each segmented into 0.71 mm parts

Analysing Dell Inc.'s Adoption of Just-in-Time Manufacturing. (Academic)


The objective of this project is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of Dell Inc.'s adoption of just-in-time (JIT) manufacturing practices. Key facets to be explored include the rationale behind Dell's decision to adopt JIT, the implementation process, associated challenges encountered, and the resultant benefits achieved. By delving into these aspects, the project seeks to provide insights into how Dell's embrace of JIT has influenced its production efficiency, inventory management, cost reduction strategies, and overall competitiveness within the technology industry landscape.
Key insights from the research:

  • Elements of JIT at Dell Inc: Dell Inc is known to be the first computer hardware to pioneer in JIT system, by its own JIT element strategies. Elements such like: 1. Direct Sales Model, 2. Built to Order, 3. Vendor Managed Inventory, 4. Push and Pull Strategy and 5. Cash on Hand Model.
  • Benefits of JIT at Dell Inc:
    1. Cost reduction by cutting down the capital tied up invetment in stocks.
    2. Multivendor Sourcing,
    3. Reduced Lead Time.
